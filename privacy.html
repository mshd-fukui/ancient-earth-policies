Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ancient Earth | KamiyaNitro ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our content publishing application.

1. Information We Collect

Our application collects only the minimum information required for OAuth 2.0 authentication with TikTok, including your TikTok user ID, username, and access tokens. We do not collect personal data beyond what is necessary for publishing content to your authorized TikTok account.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

— Authenticate with TikTok's Content Posting API
— Publish video content to the authorized TikTok account
— Monitor the publishing status of uploaded content

3. Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored securely as encrypted environment variables within our private GitHub repository. We do not store tokens in publicly accessible locations. Access tokens are automatically refreshed and old tokens are discarded.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user information with third parties. Data is transmitted only between our application and TikTok's official API endpoints.

5. Data Retention

We retain OAuth tokens only for as long as necessary to maintain the automated publishing service. Tokens can be revoked at any time by disconnecting the application from your TikTok account settings.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke our application's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's privacy settings. Upon revocation, all stored tokens are invalidated.

7. Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository or TikTok account @ancientearth.kamiyanitro.
